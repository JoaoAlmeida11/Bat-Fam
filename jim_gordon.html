Jim Gordon

This is my dear friend Jim Gordon!

Jim Gordon is the commissioner of the Gotham Police Department. Gordon shares my deep commitment to put an end to crime in Gotham. An exceptional person!

Before Gordon became the comissioner of the GCPD, he served in the United States´ Marine Corp and he was an excellent military. Gordon and I are allies when it comes to fighting crime... I trust him, and he trusts me. Whenever there is some kind of trouble, Gordon calls me through a "Bat-signal" (Yup, I have my own signal and you don´t, ehehe.)

An honorable man!. If you want to know more about Gordon´s history check out this video: History Of Commissioner Jim Gordon!.

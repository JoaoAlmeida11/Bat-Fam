Two - Face

This guy used to be good...

Harvey Dent, known as Two - Face, was once an outstanding Gotham City District Attorney, who also wished to put an end to crime and corruption in Gotham.

However, Harvey hid a dark side due to his being abused by his father as a child. Developing schizophrenia his dual personality remained hidden. Until the moment he could not hide his true identity anymore. Harvey suffered a terrible accident that mutilated half of his face.

I feel pity for him...I really do. If you want to know more about Two-Face´s origin and history check out this video: Two-Face: Origins.

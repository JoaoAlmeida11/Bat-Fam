Penguin

Oswald Chesterfield Cobblepot, also known as The Penguin, was once a child of a wealthy family. He was bullied due to his short height, obese figure and bird like nose. He had no friends.

Oswald´s overprotective mother forced him to always carry an umbrella. Oswald is called "The Penguin" due to his obession with birds.

He became angry with the world because he was never accepted. As a result he took up crimes as a means to gain power and to take revenge to those who had deared to sneer at his apperance.

What a crazy person! If you want to know more about Penguin´s origin and history check out this video: The Penguin: Origins and History.

Alfred Pennyworth

Alfred Pennyworth is my butler. He always helps me whenever I need. For that reason, I regard him as one of my greatest friends.

Alfred took care of me, after my parents´ death.

I also had trouble at school, for I was bullied. Alfred helped me to sort it out.

Our History

When I met Alfred he was a rotund and bowler-hatted that had just crossed the ocean. Soon he became my butler. And soon, he discovered the Batcave, which is to say he found out my secret!

As time went by, Alfred changed his personality, but he was always the steadfast, loyal and utterly indispensable behind-the-scenes helper to the Caped Crusader: me!

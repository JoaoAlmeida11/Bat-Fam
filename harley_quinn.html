Harley Quinn

This former psychiatrist is the on-again off-again girlfriend and accomplice of the Joker.

Dr. Harleen Quinzel, also known as Harley Quinn, was once an intern psychiatrist at Arkham Asylum. While working at the asylum, she quickly found herself fascinated with the Joker and wished to unconver his insane motives.

Sympathetic and taken by his unsual charm, Harleen found herself in love with the Clown Prince of Crime. She helped the Joker to escape. She joined the world of crime, under the name Harley Quinn.

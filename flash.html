The Flash

This guy is fast, the fastest in the world!

Flash´s real name is Barry Allen. He was a scientist. One night, while working late, a lightning bolt hit his lab. Barry was drenched in an array of chemicals. Miraculousy, he emerged untouched but not unchanged.

Barry soon discovered the accident had somehow given him super speed.

This was how Barry Allen became The Flash, the world´s fastest person! If you want to know more about Flash´s history check out this video: Superhero Origins: The Flash

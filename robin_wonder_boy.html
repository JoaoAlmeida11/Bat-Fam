Robin, the Boy Wonder

Dick Grayson

Robin is the title that I give to my loyal sidekick.

I've had many Robins by my side, each one of them special at their own way.

It all started with this young boy named Dick Grayson. He performed acrobatics with his parents at a circus. Then, one night it all changed... I saw his parents die and his determination to find the man who killed them. He reminded me of myself, you know... So I took him in and trained him to be my partner in crime (-fighting, of course). He then took the name Robin, as her mother used to call him.

Later he joined a group of Teen Titans, and then, after some time, he wanted to become independent in this business of crime-fighting, so he became Nightwing. I'm so proud of him!

If you want to see more of him, about our adventures and where he is now, you can check this video: Nightwing: The First Robin! (Dick Grayson) [Batman].

Jason Todd - the Rebel

So Jason was a troubled kid when I met him. He was trying to steal some parts of my Batmobil when I found him. Needless to say, I took him in. He had potential to become the next Robin. It took 6 months of training. He was a great fighter, but he wasn't anything like Dick... Sure, the kid was strong and skilled, he had this spark in his eyes, but unlike Dick, Jason was moved by anger. He was reckless and disobedient, violent even!

You see, Jason was a great Robin and all great things must come to an end... So Joker put up this trap to get to me and (with a little help from the Batfans - thanks a lot...) he caught Jason. He then proceeded to beat him to the ground with a crowbar and left him there to die. I was so close to save him, but the place where he was in blew up... Joker had also planted a bomb there (again, thanks a lot Batfans, Jeez). So there I was, with Jason's corpse in my arms, almost crying the death of my son... It was a dark day. But of course shenanigans and stuff and Jason was back alive! And he was also very angry.

He then became Red Hood, and if you want to see more of him and try to figure out how this is possible, you can check out this video here: History of Red Hood! (Jason Todd) [Batman].

Tim Drake - The Detective

After Jason's... trick... I confess that I was heading down a dark path. Luckily, this kid showed up: Tim Drake. He wanted to be the very best, like no one ever was. (No, not a Pokémon master! Pay attention!) He was an acrobat, just like Dick, and really good at it. He was also very smart! He almost uncovered my secret identity all by himself!

So one day he decided to steal from Penguin's bank account and give some money for the poor (like Robin Hood). Of course Penguin didn't like that "prank". He didn't like that AT ALL. And so he sent his men to kill Tim's family. Of course I saved the day, after all I'm Batman, and he actually helped me! Then I decided to let him become Robin, but only after I trained him physical an mentally (I didn't want the "Jason thing" to happen again).

Tim got a new Robin outfit. The old one just reminded us of Jason.

After some time his father convinced him to step out because being Robin is "too dangerous"... Pfff! Come on! He's fighting by my side! How cool is that?! It's not like I would let anything happen to the boy! So Stephanie Brown, Tim's girlfriend (who also happened to be The Spoiler) stepped in to fill Robin's position. But she was stubborn as hell and liked to NOT do as I said! So I got rid of her... Which means I fired her! Gosh! I'm not THAT bad! Luckily I managed to get Tim back on board with me! But then there was this little misunderstanding where I apparently "died"... Tim never believed that and so he became the new vigilante, Red Robin.

He joined a group of Teen Titans and when I came back, things were a bit different... and I had no Robin once more. Again you can check his story right here: History of Tim Drake! (Red Robin) [Batman].

Damian Wayne - My Son

Yes, I have a son. Long story short, I had an affair with Talia Al Ghul (yes, the daughter of Ra's Al Ghul) and she got pregnant. I DIDN'T KNOW THAT! But apparently she managed to get the baby on an artificial womb in order to genetically modify him to be really strong and fast and she trained him to be the perfect killing machine! Then when he turned 10 and his training was complete, she sent him over to ME! And the kid was like "Hello father, I thought you would be taller..." Well boy, I didn't even know you existed until literally now!! What a way to make a first impression...

But anyways, she sent him over to my care for him to disturb me and my work. Little did she know that she had already done half of my job to train him to become the new Robin! Ah, jokes on you Talia! And the kid liked to be Robin! We bonded there! And I was happy to have a son. Unfortunately, Talia is crazy and she got really mad by seeing ME bond with MY SON Damian, so she put a bounty on his head and started creating these monsters with his DNA... She eventually made a clone of Damian who was bigger and stronger than him. I tried to keep Damian away from all of it, but the kid didn't listen... He got in a fight with his evil clone and... The beast killed him.

Again, I was too late.

Then things get a little complicated... But in the end I end up bringing my son back to life and he's my current Robin.

You can se how that all plays out in here: Robin: The Son of Batman! (Damian Wayne) or here How Damian Wayne Came Back To Life.

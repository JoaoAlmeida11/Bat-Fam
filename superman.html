The Man of Steel

Ah Superman... The perfect Super-Hero. Everything about him is just perfect...

Superman was born on the advanced planet of Krypton, to parents Jor-El and Lara Lor-Van,Superman´s real name is actually Kal-el. He was sent to Earth, after his father uncovered that Krypton was going to be destroyed.

Superman was found by Martha and Jonathan Kent, in Smallville. The childless couple adopted the young boy and named him Clark Kent. Superman started to develop his powers and became one of the greatest super-heros, after me of course, ehehe!

Lois Lane

Lois Lane, Superman´s beloved. Yeah I know Superman has a stable love life and I don´t. Bla bla bla...

Lois is an award-winning journalist for the Metropolis newspaper "The Daily Planet". She is in love with Superman´s alter-ego Clark Kent. Although she doesn´t know that Superman is Clark Kent, she admires the hero.

Lois and Clark have a baby called Jon Kent (the current Super Boy).

Metropolis

Metropolis, the city of Superman.

Metropolis is a major metropolitan city in the northeast region of the continental United States. It is a huge cultural center, and is highly advanced, technologically. Metropolis has state of the art services of all sorts.

The city is home to the Daily Planet building, where Superman, I mean, Clark Kent works. Its image is well-known due to the gigantic, rotating golden globe on top. Other famous buildings include the LexCorp tower and the WGBS building.
